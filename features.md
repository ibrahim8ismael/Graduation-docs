## 1. CEO Dashboard (Financial Command Center)

### Overview
Provides complete visibility into the financial health and performance of the e-commerce business.

### Features

#### A. Financial Overview
- **Total Revenue:** Sum of all completed orders within selected date range.
- **Total Orders:** Count of confirmed orders.
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** Total revenue / total orders.

#### B. Performance Highlights
- **Top Performing Day:** Day with the highest number of orders.
- **Worst Performing Day:** Day with the lowest number of orders.
- **Profit Margin Trend:** Track changes in profit margin over time.
- **Top Performing Category:**
  - Highest number of orders
  - Highest generated revenue
  - Highest repeat purchase rate
- **Underperforming Category:**
  - Lowest sales volume


#### C. Machine Learning Forecasts
- **Revenue Forecast (Next 12 Months):** Time-series forecasting based on historical data.

#### D. Campaign Analytics
- **Campaign Revenue:** Total revenue generated during the campaign.
- **Campaign Net Profit:** Revenue minus campaign cost.
- **Cost per Acquisition (CPA):** Campaign cost / number of new customers gained.
- **Return on Investment (ROI):** (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.

## 2. Product Manager Dashboard (Product Intelligence Workspace)

### Overview
Gives full insight into product and Category performance and sales trends.

### Features

#### A. Product Performance Analysis
- **Top Performing Category:**
  - Highest number of orders
  - Highest generated revenue
  - Highest repeat purchase rate
- **Underperforming Category:**
  - Lowest sales volume

#### B.  Trend Analytics
- **Sales Trend per Category:** View monthly/weekly performance for each Category.
- **Inventory Alerts:** Notify when stock seems low.

#### C. Predictive Analytics
- **Product Demand Forecasting:** Predict future demand to support decision-making.
- **Product Performance Prediction:** Identify products likely to rise or fall in popularity.

## 3. Marketer Dashboard (Campaign & Customer Growth Hub)

### Overview
Tracks marketing campaign performance and customer acquisition metrics.

### Features

#### A. Campaign Management
- Create campaigns with:
  - Name
  - Date range
  - Target product(s)
  - Campaign cost
  - Promotion details

#### B. Campaign Analytics
- **Campaign Revenue:** Total revenue generated during the campaign.
- **Campaign Net Profit:** Revenue minus campaign cost.
- **Cost per Acquisition (CPA):** Campaign cost / number of new customers gained.
- **Return on Investment (ROI):** (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.

#### C. Customer Insights
- **New Customers:** Number of first-time buyers during campaign.
- **Returning Customers:** Existing customers who made purchases due to the campaign.
- **Customer Conversion Rate:** Orders / total campaign reach (if data available).

#### D. Product-Level Effectiveness
- Best-selling products during the campaign.
- Products with highest conversion.
- Products most impacted by the promotion.
